About Me

Hello, I’m Benjamin Weaver, an experienced educator with 11 years of teaching under my belt. Currently in my 5th year teaching 4th grade, I am passionate about educating young minds. Originally hailing from Brownsburg, Indiana, I pursued my Education degree at IUPUI and graduated in 2012.

I am not just a teacher but also a proud husband and father of three wonderful children aged 12, 5, and 2. Our home is a lively one, hosting a diverse array of pets including a bearded dragon, a tortoise, a tarantula, two sugar gliders, an axolotl, shrimp, chickens, and a lovable dog.

My journey into the realm of programming began when I taught a computer science class during online learning. Intrigued by the possibilities, I started exploring different facets of technology. Building my own computers became a hobby, and I delved into programming through creating interactive tools using Google Sheets for my students. As my interest grew, I even developed a no-code app for my class game, but the cost prompted me to take matters into my own hands.

This blog serves as a documentation of my evolving journey into programming and development. I am excited to share my experiences, challenges, and triumphs as I learn new things in this dynamic field. Additionally, I aim to consolidate my portfolio in one place, providing a comprehensive overview of my growth and achievements. Join me on this exciting venture of discovery and development!
