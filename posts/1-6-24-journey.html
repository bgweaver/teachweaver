My Learning Journey in Programming and Development

Posted by Ben Weaver on Jan 6th, 2024

As an elementary school teacher, my passion for education has always driven me to explore new ways to engage my students and enhance their learning experience. Recently, I have taken a keen interest in programming and development, which has opened up a whole new world of possibilities for me and my students.

It all started when I began teaching Computer Science during online learning. I realized the importance of introducing them to the basics of coding and computational thinking. After developing my interest by learning to teach, I challenged myself to continue exploring the field. I wanted to gain a better understanding of how software and applications are created, and how I could leverage this knowledge to create educational tools for my students.

During my journey, I stumbled upon the concept of no-code development using Google Sheets. This excited me because it allowed individuals with no prior coding experience to build functional applications. Intrigued by the possibilities, I was able to create an exciting tool for students to access my class game, Cloudtop Spire. They were able to manage their character, their inventory, and even take on extra challenges when they were finished with other work. Robert Petito has a great YouTube series on gamifying Glide Apps if this is something you're interested in trying.

Creating the no-code app was a great experience, but I was limited by the constraints of the free tool. As my app continued to evolve and no-code sites became more tailored to businesses and larger teams, and thus less affordable, I began to look for other ways to create my vision.

I decided to document my learning journey by creating this website. Here, I aim to collect and showcase the different parts and projects that I have undertaken throughout my exploration of programming and development.

Through this website, I hope to inspire other educators and individuals who are interested in programming to embark on their own learning journeys. I believe everyone has the potential to learn and excel in this field, regardless of their background or prior experience.

As I continue to learn and grow in the world of programming and development, I am eager to share my experiences, challenges, and insights with others. I will share tutorials, tips, and resources that I have found helpful along the way to make programming more accessible and enjoyable for all.

So, whether you are a fellow educator, a student, or simply someone curious about programming, I invite you to join me on this exciting journey. Together, we can explore the vast landscape of programming and development, and discover the endless possibilities it holds for education and beyond.
